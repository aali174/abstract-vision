Title: Water inspired

Medium: Acrylic and oil paint on canvas

Date:10/2017

Size: 16x20

This painting features a series of circular and oval shapes, each outlined in a bold white line, wanting to create a stark contrast against the rich, dark colors within. The interior of these shapes is painted with deep greens, reds, and browns with brush strokes that convey movement and energy. The use of color is both harmonious and intense; the dark tones are reminiscent of natural elements like wood, leaves, or stones while the white outlines give the image a modern, graphic quality.
