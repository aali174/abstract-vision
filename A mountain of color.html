Title: A mountain of color

Medium: Soft/chalk pastels on canvas

Date:08/2018

Size: 16x20

This painting features various elements that are painted in different hues, such as red, blue, green, purple, and more. Wanting the painting to have a textural quality, making the surface to be rough with raised lines creating patterns across the colors. Having the painting look like it has faint traces of text or print that have been painted over, adding a layer of mystery and intrigue.
