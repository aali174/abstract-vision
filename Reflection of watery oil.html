Title: Reflection of watery oil

Medium: Acrylic, water and oil paint on canvas

Date:09/2022

Size: 16x20

This painting is rich with a mix of dark and vibrant colors, blending and contrasting in a dance of emotion and expression. The strokes are bold, the textures varied, creating a sense of depth and movement. This painting depicts a stormy night with wild as the wind. In the midst of this storm. The dark greys and deep purples represent the tumultuous emotions; they are the storm that rages within. Yet amidst this chaos, splashes of green and yellow emerge like rays of hope piercing through dark clouds.
