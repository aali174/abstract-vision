Title: Broken flower

Medium: Acrylic paint on canvas

Date:06/2022

Size: 16x20

This painting featuring intricate line art and a warm color palette. The painting predominantly features warm tones of red, orange, and hints of yellow. There is a complex, intricate line drawing in black that resembles a blooming flower at the center. The background has a textured appearance with variations in color intensity and some areas of the paint that are more diluted.
